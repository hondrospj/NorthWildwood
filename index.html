Datum reference

NAVD88 is a land elevation datum used for surveyed ground heights and flood mapping.

MLLW means Mean Lower Low Water, a tide based datum commonly used for marine water levels.

In this app, MLLW is shown as NAVD88 + 2.75 ft.
